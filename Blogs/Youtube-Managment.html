Here Is why you need Youtube management for your channel

25 January 2022 / Youtube

You're a Youtuber in 2022 and you're looking to grow your channel, get sponsors and get monetized as fast as possible, but your channel growth is not quite what you thought it would be. View and subscriptions are a major part of Youtube and most new channels get very little of both, as a Youtuber looking to grow your channel you should know all of the elements that have a part in getting your channel to where you want it to be.

To make sure you have significant growth for your channel, there are a number of things that you have to consider when you are working towards that goal these include:

  • Channel Name
    • Your channel name should be relevant to what your content is about
  • Subscription Optimisation
    • This inlcudes a number of tweeks inlcuding a suggestion to subscribe on the channel cover and in the videos
  • Customise Channel Appearance
    • Your channel should never be basic!
  • Add your social Links
    • Your subscribers need to know who you are
  • Add a featured video
    • You want visitors to know what kind of content you're making
  • Share your channel content
    • Share your content on social media or employ the use of Social Media Marketing to help grow your channel

    However these are not the only requirements to channel growth there are more technical tweeks that significantly help with getting more view and subscribers

    Channel Description
    YouTube and Google can’t watch or listen to your video. So having a well optimized and written description is very important for these palytforms to understand what your video is about.
    + YouTube allows 5,000 words in the description, so don’t be afraid to add. Give a detailed description with supporting information.
    + Your description helps your video rank for long keywords
    + Providing links back to your YouTube channel, as well as encouraging viewers to “like”, "Subscribe" and leave a comment (if relevant to your content), is also recommended.
    + Use your keywords naturally and be careful not to “keyword stuff”

    Tags
    Tags are your chance to tell YouTube which keywords and phrases you think are relevant to the video. Therefore it’s best to include the search terms with the highest volume (including brand terms). While you can include a large number of tags, it’s important to only use the ones which are important, including a large number of irrelevant tags is likely to result in reduced views, comments and subscribers and reduced performance ratings. This is also relevant in regard to the video title. When deciding on tags, it is key to think about what terms people will search when looking for your video.

    Thumbnails
    Thumbnails are very important and have a direct influence on clicks. Since YouTube traffic comes not only as a result of ranking well, but also relies on users browsing and clicking related content, it's important to have an engaging custom thumbnail that does not reveal too much content about the video to attract as many clicks as possible. It's a good idea for a thumbnail image to also contain some text or a message which goes well with the title of the video. This is applicable when the call to action phrase doesn't have any search volume but is simply there to 'engage', in other words increase the chances of users clicking through to the relevant videos.

    Category
    Categorizing your video correctly helps visitors find the video easily through YouTube’s internal search, related videos and the category browser within YouTube.
